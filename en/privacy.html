Privacy Policy

ClockIn for Jira · Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Fuksia Labs ("we") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the ClockIn for Jira app ("the App"), distributed through the Atlassian Marketplace and installed on Jira Cloud sites.

1. Data controller

The data controller for the processing described in this policy is:

Fuksia Labs (Sergi Panisello, sole proprietor)
Contact: info@fuksia.com
Spain

2. What data we collect

The App operates within the Atlassian Forge environment. The data we process is strictly necessary for the core function of work-time tracking and compliance:

DataSourcePurpose
Atlassian account identifier (accountId)Jira Cloud (via Atlassian API)Identify the user logging their working time
Display name and email addressJira Cloud (via Atlassian API)Show the user's name in the interface and team reports
Clock events (start, pause, resume, stop) with timestampsEntered by the user when using the AppCalculate worked hours and generate the legally required time-tracking record
Incidents and comments about time recordsEntered by the user or their managerManage corrections to time records
Company configuration (work schedule, user role)Entered by the organization's administratorApply the schedule rules configured by the company

3. Where data is stored

All data is stored exclusively through Atlassian's Forge Storage service, within Atlassian Cloud infrastructure. Fuksia Labs does not operate its own servers or external databases to store this data. Atlassian applies its own security and compliance measures, described in its Privacy Policy.

4. Legal basis for processing

The processing of this data is based on:

  • Performance of a contract — time tracking is the core function of the App, contracted by the organization that installs it.
  • Compliance with a legal obligation — working-time recording is a legal obligation for companies in Spain (Royal Decree-Law 8/2019) and in other European jurisdictions with similar regulations.

5. Data retention

Time records are retained for the period required by applicable labor law (in Spain, a minimum of 4 years under Article 34.9 of the Workers' Statute). Records are not physically deleted; deletions requested by the user are marked as such while the history is retained for audit purposes, without being shown in the user interface.

6. Who we share data with

We do not share, sell, or disclose data to third parties for commercial or advertising purposes. Data is visible only to:

  • The user themselves, regarding their own records.
  • Users with manager or administrator roles within the same organization, according to the role configuration set by the administrator.
  • Atlassian, as the infrastructure provider for the Forge Storage service the App runs on.

7. Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, object to, or request portability of your data. Since the App operates within your organization, we recommend contacting your Jira site administrator first. You may also contact us directly at info@fuksia.com.

8. App uninstallation

If the organization's administrator uninstalls the App, data stored in Forge Storage is removed in accordance with Atlassian Forge's data retention policies following uninstallation.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the App or applicable regulations. The date of the latest update is shown at the top of this document.

10. Contact

For any questions about this policy or how your data is processed, write to us at info@fuksia.com.
